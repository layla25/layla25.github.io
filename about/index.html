About me

My name is Layla. I choose this English name due to the album of Eric Clapton, but only one of my friends realized that.. Anyway.

My hometown is Shanghai. I was obsessed by the movies of Kar Wai Wong in my high school, so I came to Hong Kong for college. Actually, the two cities are quite similar in some way. And..maybe I will set out for somewhere else after college.

I love arts, and since I got my Ipad with apple pencil, I started to learn digital illustration. I put some of my painting in my blog, you can check the activities tag.

I play piano, but I also like rock and roll.
